## Synthesis workflow

1. Export all post-interview forms into comparison matrix
2. Tag standout quotes by theme (pain, workflow, demo reaction, pricing, AI concern)
3. Calculate hypothesis pass/fail per [hypotheses.md](../../specs/hypotheses.md)
4. Plot Van Westendorp curves per region (spreadsheet or manual band overlap)
5. Draft regional summaries; identify beachhead candidate
6. Select highlight clips: strongest pain quotes, positive demo reactions, pricing insights

## Exit criteria

All five outcome documents complete; clear per-region patterns documented.
