## Outreach message template

> Hi [Name] — we're building a tool to help nail salon owners stay on top of trends and create social content faster. We're looking for salon owners to share their experience in a 45-minute video call (compensated with a gift card + free pilot access). Would you be open to a short chat about how you manage Instagram/TikTok for your salon?

Customize per region and platform. For Vietnam, send in Vietnamese when possible.

## Screener pass criteria

- Owns or manages a nail salon / nail spa
- Posts on social media ≥2 times per month for business
- In business ≥1 year
- Willing to participate in a recorded 45-minute interview
