# Interview Execution Kit

Everything needed to run M3 interviews. Operational work happens outside the repo; artifacts are stored here.

## Before each interview

1. Assign code from [README.md](../README.md) tracker
2. Send [pre-interview form](../questionnaires/pre-interview.md)
3. Pre-run demo reports for participant's region ([demo-script.md](../specs/demo-script.md))
4. Open [mock-social-post.json](../../../samples/market-validation/mock-social-post.json)
5. Start recording (Zoom/Loom); test audio

## During interview

| Segment | Guide section | Duration |
|---------|---------------|----------|
| Consent + warm-up | A | 10 min |
| Workflow | B | 15 min |
| Pain + demo | C | 10 min |
| Content concepts | D | 10 min |
| Pricing | E | 5 min |
| Close | F | 5 min |

Use language-appropriate guide:

- [English](../guides/interview-guide-en.md)
- [Vietnamese](../guides/interview-guide-vi.md)
- [Finnish](../guides/interview-guide-fi.md)

## Video highlight criteria (for M4)

Select clips where interviewee:

- Describes pain vividly (time, inspiration, consistency)
- Reacts positively to trend demo
- Names a specific price or WTP band
- Expresses AI authenticity concern
- Describes current workflow in detail
