## A. Warm-up & context (10 min)

**Goal:** Understand their business and current social presence.

1. Tell me about your salon — size, location, how long you've been open.
2. Which social platforms do you use for business? How often do you post?
3. Who creates your social content today? (You, staff, agency, no one)
4. What does a "good month" vs "bad month" look like for your social presence?

**Probe:** Bookings from social? DM volume? Competitor pressure?

---

## B. Current workflow — trend discovery & content (15 min)

**Goal:** Document how they find trends and create posts today (H3).

5. Walk me through how you last decided what nail design or theme to promote.
6. Where do you find inspiration or trends? (IG, TikTok, Pinterest, peers, magazines, courses)
7. How much time per week do you spend on social media — creating, posting, and engaging?
8. What is the hardest part of keeping your profiles active and relevant?
9. Have you tried any tools? (Canva, CapCut, ChatGPT, scheduling apps) What worked or didn't?

**Probe:** Do they plan content in advance or post spontaneously?

---

## C. Pain validation + demo (10 min)

**Goal:** Validate H1, H2; get product reaction.

10. Rate 1–5: How painful is staying on-trend while running the salon?
11. What happens when you fall behind on posting? (Bookings, DMs, morale, competitor pressure)
12. If you had a weekly "trend briefing" tailored to your salon style, would you use it? Why or why not?

### Demo

Follow [demo-script.md](../../specs/demo-script.md):

13. Show neutral + personalized trend report. What feels useful? What feels wrong or generic?
14. Would citation links and reference images help you trust and use the trends?

---

## D. Social content generation concepts (10 min)

**Goal:** Validate H5; test mock post concepts.

15. Show mock: trend → suggested caption + hashtags + posting tip.

Reference [content-generation-concepts.md](../../specs/content-generation-concepts.md) cards A–D as needed.

16. For **captions/text**: prefer AI draft you edit, templates only, or write yourself?
17. For **visuals**: prefer your own nail photos + AI suggestions, AI mood boards only, or stock/reference images?
18. Rate 1–5: likelihood you'd publish AI-assisted content vs only your own photos.
19. Any concerns about AI content? (Authenticity, client trust, platform rules, language)

---

## E. Willingness to pay — price ranges (5 min)

**Goal:** Validate H4. Ask in local currency. **Do not ask them to invent a price** — show the ranges below and collect reactions.

> We're exploring a monthly subscription for trend research and content drafts.

**Show these monthly ranges** (use the column for their region):

| Range | Vietnam (VND) | Finland (EUR) | International (USD) |
|-------|---------------|---------------|---------------------|
| A | Under 200,000 | Under €15 | Under $15 |
| B | 200,000–400,000 | €15–25 | $15–25 |
| C | 500,000–900,000 | €35–50 | $35–55 |
| D | 1,200,000+ | €70+ | $79+ |

20. For each range A–D, what's your gut reaction? (**too cheap** / **good value** / **expensive but maybe** / **too expensive**)
21. Which range feels like the best fit for what you saw in the demo — and why?
22. At that "best fit" range, what would need to be included for it to feel worth paying?
23. Would you prefer monthly subscription, per-report, or bundled with other salon software?

Record reactions in [pricing-van-westendorp.md](../questionnaires/pricing-van-westendorp.md).

---

## F. Close (5 min)

24. On a scale 0–10, how likely are you to try a free 30-day pilot?
25. Who else should we talk to?
26. May we follow up when we launch?

**Thank them.** Remind about incentive (gift card + pilot access).
