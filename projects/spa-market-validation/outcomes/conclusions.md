## Pre-validation desk conclusions

_From competitor research and ICP persona (before interviews):_

1. **Gap exists:** No tool combines web-backed nail trend research, personalization, and post-ready content in one salon workflow.
2. **Substitutes are manual:** Owners scroll IG/TikTok and use Canva/ChatGPT ad hoc — fragmented, time-consuming.
3. **Trust matters:** Citations and reference images may differentiate Willbe from generic AI.
4. **Content MVP should be conservative:** Caption + hashtags from trends, owner supplies photos — aligns with authenticity concerns in persona research.
5. **Regional nuance expected:** VN needs Vietnamese and Facebook/Zalo awareness; FI smaller market but potentially higher EUR WTP.
