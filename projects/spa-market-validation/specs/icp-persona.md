# ICP Persona — Nail Salon Owner

Primary interview target for market validation. Not a fictional character — a composite profile to guide recruitment and question framing.

## Profile

| Attribute | Typical range |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Role** | Owner-operator or manager of a nail salon / nail spa |
| **Business age** | 1–10 years |
| **Team size** | Solo artist to 5 technicians |
| **Location** | Urban or suburban; Vietnam (HCMC, Hanoi), Finland (Helsinki, Tampere), or international (US, UK, AU, etc.) |
| **Revenue driver** | Walk-ins + bookings via DM, phone, or booking apps |
| **Social responsibility** | Owner does it themselves, or delegates to a senior tech — rarely a dedicated marketer |

## Name: **Lan** (archetype)

Lan runs a 3-chair nail studio in Ho Chi Minh City. She's been open for four years. Instagram and Facebook bring in about 30% of new clients. She posts 2–4 times a week when she's not too busy, but during peak season she goes quiet for weeks. She scrolls TikTok and Pinterest at night for inspiration, saves posts to a folder, and sometimes copies trending designs for her menu board. She's tried Canva for stories but finds captions the hardest part. She doesn't have time to research what's "in" each month — she relies on what clients ask for and what she sees other salons posting.

## Goals

- Stay visible on social without spending hours every day
- Show work that feels current and professional, not dated
- Attract new clients and re-engage existing ones
- Build a recognizable salon style (clean girl, chrome, art-heavy, etc.)

## Frustrations

- **Time:** Clients come first; social media happens after hours
- **Inspiration gap:** Unsure what's trending vs what's already passé
- **Consistency:** Posting bursts then silence hurts algorithm reach
- **Skills:** Good at nails, not copywriting or graphic design
- **Language:** (VN) Wants Vietnamese captions that sound natural, not robotic
- **Authenticity:** Worried AI or stock content won't feel like "her" salon

## Current tools & behaviors

| Activity | Typical approach |
|----------|------------------|
| Trend discovery | Scroll IG/TikTok, follow nail educators, Pinterest boards, peer salons |
| Photo capture | Phone photos at the chair; occasional ring light |
| Editing | Instagram filters, Canva, CapCut for reels |
| Captions | Write herself, copy/adapt from others, sometimes skip captions |
| Scheduling | Post manually; rarely use Later/Buffer |
| AI | May have tried ChatGPT casually; skeptical of quality for beauty |

## What success looks like for Willbe

- Weekly digest of 5–8 trends relevant to her style and region
- Each trend with a photo reference, short description, and editable caption + hashtags
- 15 minutes to turn a trend into a post using her own nail photos
- Feels confident the trends are current (citations help)
- Price feels fair vs hiring a part-time social helper

## Recruitment fit

**Include if:**

- Owns or manages a nail-focused business
- Posts ≥2×/month on at least one platform for business
- In business ≥1 year

**Exclude if:**

- Full-service spa with no nail focus
- Agency or educator only (not operating a salon)
- No social presence at all
